LUDDITE

In Spy Game, you have been tasked with finding out what lies behind the LUDDITE cult, a sinister group that was founded by Harold in the wake of the Y2K fear, with the group teaching their followers to embrace the anti-technology ways and severely punishing followers that use technology. All the spy game missions are connected to the storyline of Luddite, but they do not have to be played in sequence.

MISSION 1: PURIFY

(Recommended for 1st timer

The cult has been offering their followers water that has been drugged with substances, making it hard for the followers to think straight and are trusting the teachings of the cult more strongly. You are outsiders who are aware of this illicit act and want to help the poor followers who have fallen victim to this cult. You have been tasked to PURIRY the water storage system in the Luddite cult base by infusing it with an antidote

*For better game immersion, kindly bring along your wired or wireless earphone/headset.

Your Mission
Purify the drugged water

Physical
Light climbing & balancing
